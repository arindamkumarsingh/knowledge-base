# Purpose

Soc teams main purpose is detect and response, the solution is centralised monitoring of the organisation network

* detect vuln = vuln is a weakness which a hacker can exploit beyond their perminssion level. Any system or software can have vulnerabilities, SOC can discover the vulnerabillities and inform what needs to be patched to fix the vulnerability.

* detect unauthorised activity

* detect policy violations - like downloading pirated media or sort.

* detect intrusion - intrusion is unauthorised access to system and networks, like exploiting network.

**RESPONSE** 

response includes performing a full scan and seeing the rootcause of the incident

### Three pillars of SOC

People, process and technology coexist in soc environment.
